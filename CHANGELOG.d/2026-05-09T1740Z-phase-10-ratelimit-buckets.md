### Added

- `apps/server/internal/ratelimit`: register two Phase 10 token-bucket configs ahead of their wiring PRs. `WrapsNeededUserConfig()` (default `Burst=10` / `Refill=1m`, env `CHAT_WRAPS_NEEDED_BURST` / `CHAT_WRAPS_NEEDED_REFILL`) is the per-user bucket for `GET /api/channels/{id}/members/wraps-needed`; `ReplayWrapConfig()` (default `Burst=3` / `Refill=5m`, env `CHAT_REPLAY_WRAP_BURST` / `CHAT_REPLAY_WRAP_REFILL`) is the per-`(channel_id, member_user_id)` bucket for `POST /api/channels/{id}/members/{user_id}/replay-wrap`. Defaults pinned in the e2e-encryption decision log (L31, L35, L36). Both shapes mirror the existing `ChannelWriteUserConfig*` / `DMWriteUserConfig*` / `ReadMarkUserConfig*` pattern. No HTTP handler is wired in this PR — wiring lands in the wraps-needed and replay-wrap sub-issues.
